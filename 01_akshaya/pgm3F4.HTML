DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

Our college understood the need of a professional computer course in this Era of Information Technology and applied for the BCA and M.Sc Computer Science programme and University sanctioned BCA course in 2013 and M.Sc CS in 2015.The program focuses on theoretical computer science as well as software and application development. We afford our students a strong foundation in the principles of Computer Science and offer a wide range of courses in cutting-edge areas such as Machine Learning, Computational Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Programming languages, Software engineering, Network and security and Theory of Computation.
