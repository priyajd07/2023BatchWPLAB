MES Engineering College

MES College MES College of Engineering is a Kuttippuram, Malappuram-based private engineering college. It is Kerala's first self-financing engineering college, with a campus that extends for more than a mile alongside the Bharathappuzha River. It was founded in 1994 as a minority-serving institution. The college is approved by AICTE and CoA and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and University of Calicut for the M. Arch programme.
